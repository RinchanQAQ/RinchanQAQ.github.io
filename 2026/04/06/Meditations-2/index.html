Meditations-2

发表于2026-04-06|更新于2026-04-06|Meditations
|浏览量:

一天开始时，就对自己说： 我今天会遇到爱多管闲事的人、忘恩负义的人、傲慢的人、欺诈的人、嫉妒的人和孤僻不合群的人。

他们之所以变成这样，是因为不懂什么是善、什么是恶。 但我不一样：我明白善恶的本质，知道善是美好、恶是丑陋；

我也知道，这些犯错的人，本性和我是同类 ——我们不只是血肉相似，更拥有同样的理性、同样分有一份神性。

所以，没有人能真正伤害我，因为没人能把恶强加于我。

我也不会对他们发怒、不会憎恨他们。

因为我们天生就是要彼此合作的，就像手和脚、唇和齿、上眼睑和下眼睑。彼此对立、互相敌视，就是违背天性，就是自寻烦恼、自我排斥。

沉思录 2：1

At the beginning of the day, say to yourself:

I will meet people who are meddlesome, ungrateful, arrogant, deceitful, envious, and unsociable.

They have become like this because they do not know what is good and what is evil.

But I am different: I understand the nature of good and evil, knowing that good is beautiful and evil is ugly.

I also know that those who do wrong share the same nature as me — we are not only similar in flesh and blood, but also possess the same reason and a share of the divine.

Therefore, no one can truly harm me, for no one can impose evil upon me.

I will not be angry with them, nor will I hate them.

For we are born to cooperate with one another, like hands and feet, lips and teeth, upper eyelids and lower eyelids.

To oppose and be hostile to each other is to go against nature, to bring trouble upon oneself, and to alienate oneself from one’s own kind.

Meditations 2:1
